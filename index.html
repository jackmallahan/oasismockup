What is Floating?

Floating is a fantastic way to relax and rejuvenate, and at Oasis Float Spa we’re committed to ensuring that your float experience is perfect, every time. We’ve designed our North Seattle float spa to make it effortless for you to chill—from our world-class float pools, to our beautiful tea service in our spacious lounge, to our highly competent and non-intrusive team members—we’re focused on giving you pure relaxation, every time.

Our Mission

Floating has really changed our lives, and our mission Is to give you the same joy of floating that we experience, every time you visit us. Come float with us today!

6 Private Float Rooms

each with its own changing area, shower, and spacious float pool. Float effortlessly in our highly salinized water and experience a spontaneous meditative state

Immaculately Clean

We use the industry’s most sophisticated filtration and purification system. The water passes through a 1-micron filter, six Corona ozone generators, a medical-quality auto-doser adds a precise amount of hydrogen peroxide, and finally filtered through a ultra-violet light chamber. By the time the client steps foot in the pool, the hydrogen peroxide has been completely consumed, leaving nothing but sparkling, pristine salt water!
