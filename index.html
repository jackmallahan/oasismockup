Welcome to Oasis Float Spa!

Floating is a fantastic way to relax and rejuvenate, and at Oasis Float Spa we’re committed to ensuring that you have a perfect float experience every time you visit us.  We have designed our float spa, located in North Seattle, with an eye towards making it effortless for you to chill out.

Floating has really changed our lives, and our mission is to give you the same joy of floating that we experience, every time you visit us.   Come float with us today!

Our Float Pods and Float Rooms

Our beautiful float pods are the largest free-standing float pods in Seattle. They are over 8½ feet long, head-to-toe. Each of our six float pods is located inside its own sound-proof room, with 9-inch thick insulated walls, solid-core doors and sound-sealed door frames. The float rooms each have their own private shower, changing area, towels, bath robe, ear plugs, water and other amenities.

Silence!

The float pods themselves are perched on top of highly-engineered sound isolation pads, eliminating any chance of street noise entering the float pod. Each float pod is also equipped with starlight ceilings that can be turned on or off by the customer, as well as underwater speakers that deliver meditative music, also controlled by the customer. In addition, we provide smartphone jacks that allow customers to listen to their own music or guided meditations.

Perfect Temp!

We have worked very hard to ensure that the temperature of the float room and the float water is just right. Our float pods have heaters built right into the floor of the float pods, ensuring that the temperature doesn’t fluctuate, which can happen in float pods that rely solely on their water filtration systems to re-heat the water between float sessions. Experienced floaters can find relaxation in any of the float tanks in Seattle, but we’re delighted to hear from many float enthusiasts that the quality of our float pods really enhances their experience.

Immaculately Clean

We use the industry’s most sophisticated filtration and purification system. The water passes through a 1-micron filter, six Corona ozone generators, a medical-quality auto-doser adds a precise amount of hydrogen peroxide, and finally filtered through a ultra-violet light chamber. By the time the client steps foot in the pool, the hydrogen peroxide has been completely consumed, leaving nothing but sparkling, pristine salt water!
